About our Academy

Shelton Martial Arts Academy is located in Shelton, Connecticut.

Our students range in age, some being as young as 7 all the way up to 50+ years of age. Our school is fully credentialed by the main governing boards of traditional and Olympic TaeKwonDo both in America and Internationally. Students therefore are able compete locally or on the international stage depending on their personal goals.

SMAA's mission is to provide our students with world-class instruction allowing them to accomplish goals they never thought of exceeding.

At SMAA students have the opportunity to go as far as their skill and drive takes them.

Why should you learn Taekwondo?

Taekwondo first oringinated in Korea during the mid-20th century, primarily developed by Korean Martial artists who combined elements of traditional Korean fighting styles like "Taekkyon" and Subak" with influences from Japanese Karate and Chinese matrial arts.

With the two different fighting styles incorperated within the style of Taekwondo, each student will be able to excel in a full body workout every lesson. This will allow them to advance themselves physically and mentally.

While learning the main techniques and abilities there are five "Tenents" of Taekwondo that is always said at the end of a class. They are the following:

Courtesy  Integrity  Perseverance  Self Control  Indomitable Spirit
