Meet our Instructors

Gabriella Castorina,

Senior Instructor | 3rd Degree Blackbelt

Gabriella has been with this organization most of her life. Being involved in the art of TaeKwonDo, it not only advanced her physcial abilities but also her mental and eomtional mindset. Gabriella has been studying the art of TaeKwonDo for 15 years and she has to say, this has been a life changer and she would recommend this sport to anybody. Continuing with TaeKwonDo from the age of nine, to present day now finishing up with college, she feel more of a well rounded individual.

In 2013, Gabriella was injrued with a broken foot and out of class for a few months. In 2020, she pulled a muscle in my chest after overworking myself which led to my body becoming physically ill. For a few months, her family and others didn't know what was wrong. In November,she was diagnosed with Hashimoto's Disease which effects the thyroid located in the front of the neck, and in front of the trachea. Gabriella never let anything stop me because her continue to push through like a bull, and deep down, she knew she is strong and the more she pushed, the stronger she became. If she can do it with all the trouble she went through, then you can too.

Jason Smith,

Senior Instructor | 4th Degree Blackbelt

After years of intense dedication and consistent practice, Jason earned his 4th-degree black belt in Taekwondo at the age of 35. This achievement signifies not only mastery of advanced techniques but also a deep understanding of the philosophies that underpin Taekwondo. Jason’s expertise includes complex forms (poomsae), precise kicks and strikes, breaking techniques, self-defense, and sparring.

His status as a 4th-degree black belt reflects his extensive experience, which includes instructing students of all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. As a Taekwondo Master, Jason has become a mentor to many aspiring martial artists, passing on his knowledge and emphasizing the values of respect, integrity, perseverance, and indomitable spirit.

Daniel Larusso,

Master Instructor | 6th Degree Blackbelt

Daniels’s upbringing in Taekwondo shaped not only his athletic career but also his worldview. He believes that martial arts, especially Taekwondo, are tools for personal growth and transformation. His philosophy centers on the idea that the true test of a martial artist is not found in victories or titles, but in the constant pursuit of self-improvement, respect for others, and the ability to overcome adversity.

Now, as a 6th Degree black belt, Daniel continues to train and compete, always striving to learn and refine his skills. He also teaches at his own Taekwondo school, Shelton Martial Arts Academy, where he is known for his patience, dedication, and ability to inspire his students. Daniel teaches not just techniques but the core values of Taekwondo that shaped his journey, believing that martial arts should enhance every aspect of life.

Daniel’s upbringing in Taekwondo has been a defining part of who he is. Through the discipline of Taekwondo, Daniel has found purpose, strength, and a sense of belonging. His story is a testament to the transformative power of martial arts, showing how perseverance, dedication, and respect can lead to success both on and off the mat.
